# VIDEO 1 — PLATFORM WALKTHROUGH

## A. Video title
**"See the Whole Web of Opportunity Around Your Mission"** — Anansi Atlas Platform Walkthrough

## B. Purpose
Give nonprofit leaders a clear, guided tour of what Anansi Atlas actually does — from entering their mission to reading their Opportunity Web Snapshot — so they understand the product well enough to apply for the pilot. Used on the website, LinkedIn featured post, outreach, email follow-up, and the signup page.

## C. Target audience
Executive directors, founders, development directors, and program leads at small-to-mid nonprofits and mission-driven orgs who feel opportunity is scattered and time is short.

## D. Main message
Opportunity around your mission is real but scattered. Anansi Atlas maps it into one clear picture — funders, partners, government pathways, resources, readiness gaps, risks, routes to capital, and next steps — so you move from scattered research to focused action.

## E. Full script / voiceover (timestamped)

**[0:00–0:10] Opening — what it is**
"Your nonprofit is surrounded by opportunity. Funders who fund your kind of work. Partners already in your corner. Doors you don't know are open yet. The problem was never the opportunity. It's that no one could see all of it at once."

**[0:10–0:22] The problem — opportunity is scattered**
"It lives in funder portals, old emails, spreadsheets, a board member's memory, a grant deadline someone forgot to write down. Scattered across a dozen places — impossible to hold in your head. So the biggest opportunities slip past the smallest teams."

**[0:22–0:34] Start with the mission**
"Anansi Atlas starts where you start — with your mission. Meet Riverside Youth Arts Collective. They put in who they serve, what they do, and where they work. That's it. No 40-field form."

**[0:34–0:48] Mapping the Opportunity Web**
"From that, Anansi Atlas maps the web of opportunity around them. Your mission sits at the center. Around it, the whole landscape organizes itself into a living map — the people, dollars, and pathways that actually connect to your work."

**[0:48–1:02] The Opportunity Web Snapshot**
"That map becomes your Opportunity Web Snapshot. It opens with a plain-language summary and a 30-day action plan — not a data dump. The first thing you see is the single most important move to make right now."

**[1:02–2:00] Walking the categories**
"Then it walks you through the whole web.
**Funders** — the foundations and grantmakers aligned to youth arts, not a generic list.
**Partners** — organizations to collaborate and share sponsorship with.
**Government** — public pathways and programs you qualify for.
**Resources** — community assets, tools, and support already within reach.
**Readiness** — an honest read on where you're strong and where you have gaps to close before you apply.
And woven through it: your strategic risks, your routes to capital, and the practical next steps — each with a deadline, each with a reason it matters."

**[2:00–2:20] How it helps leaders focus**
"On your dashboard, it comes down to one card: What To Do Next. Health scores across Readiness, Partners, Pathways, and Opportunities. Deadlines that don't slip. An active pipeline you can actually move. You stop researching. You start acting — on the right things, in the right order."

**[2:20–2:40] CTA**
"Applications are open for the Founding Atlas Partners Pilot — nineteen or twenty mission-driven organizations. Your subscription includes your Opportunity Web Snapshot, a 45-minute walkthrough, and full platform access — a founding rate of one hundred fifty dollars a month, locked for life. See the whole web of opportunity around your mission. Apply at anansiatlas.com."
