## What it is
Anansi Atlas is a **nonprofit opportunity intelligence platform** that helps nonprofits and mission-driven organizations **map the web of opportunity around their mission**. It is NOT "just grant search," not a grant-writing agency, not a guaranteed-funding solution, not a charity.

**Core offer / deliverable:** the **Opportunity Web Snapshot**.

**What it helps organizations identify:**
aligned funders · potential partners · government pathways · community resources · readiness gaps · strategic risks · routes to capital · practical next steps

**Product node vocabulary (use these exact category names):**
Funders · Partners · Government · Resources · Readiness · Pathways

## Positioning (the spine of every message)
Nonprofits are surrounded by opportunity, but it's scattered across websites, funder portals, emails, spreadsheets, relationships, local knowledge, and institutional memory. Anansi Atlas helps organizations **see the opportunity landscape around their mission clearly — so they move from scattered opportunity to focused action.**

## The campaign (right now)
- **Goal:** recruit **19–20 nonprofit / mission-driven organizations** into the **Founding Atlas Partners Pilot**.
- **Pricing (real, live):** Founding rate **$150/month, locked for life** for the first 20 orgs; **$299/month** standard after the cohort. No per-report fees, no usage limits, cancel anytime.
- **The Snapshot is NOT a free lead magnet.** It is **included in the subscription** — you join the pilot at $150/mo and the Snapshot + 45-minute walkthrough + platform access come with it. Never advertise a "free Snapshot."
- **Campaign direction:** do NOT lead with a founder-led commercial yet. First content **showcases the platform, the Opportunity Web Snapshot, and the pilot signup** — product-forward, not personal-story-forward.
- **Platform priority:** **LinkedIn first**, then Instagram and TikTok.

## Voice & tone
clear · practical · strategic · professional · warm · mission-centered · credible · direct · conversion-focused. Founder-led only when appropriate, not the main angle right now. **NOT** hype-heavy, **NOT** overly corporate, **NOT** desperate for signups, **NOT** charity-toned.

## Visual direction
Dark navy + charcoal + **gold accents**. Clean product UI shots, dashboard-style visuals, elegant motion graphics, subtle web / network / pathway visuals, a nonprofit leader working at a laptop, funder/partner mapping visuals, clean typography, smooth transitions.

**Product-accurate UI to feature in showcases (this is what the real app looks like):**
- Premium shell: **Fraunces serif** headings + Inter/DM Sans body; gold + navy palette.
- **Opportunity Web page:** an orb-web hero with pill nodes (FUNDERS, PARTNERS, GOVERNMENT, RESOURCES, READINESS, PATHWAYS) orbiting a central "YOUR MISSION" hub; an "Ecosystem Summary" with teal/gold **outlined "bubble"** value cards; a "Top Move Right Now" dark navy card.
- **Snapshot page:** leads with the summary + 30-day action plan, then funder pathways, partner pathways, top opportunities, readiness, risks & gaps; teal = strength, gold = gap color coding; rose "Due" deadline pills.
- **Dashboard:** a "What To Do Next" single-action card, KPI health scores (Readiness / Partners / Pathways / Opportunities), upcoming deadlines, active pipeline, teal opportunity "bubbles."
- **Sidebar sections:** Overview (Dashboard, Snapshot, Opportunity Web, Partners & Sponsorship) · Take Action (Pipeline, Pathways, Ecosystem) · Build Strength (Readiness, Resources) · Your Space (Organization).

## Hard "avoid" list
generic SaaS language · guaranteed funding promises · exaggerated AI claims · cartoon spider imagery · mystical/gimmicky folklore language · over-explaining Anansi · making it sound like only a grant-search tool · founder-led storytelling as the main angle right now · sounding desperate · sounding like a charity instead of a serious business-support platform.
