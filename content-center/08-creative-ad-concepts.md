## Concept 01 — "40 Tabs" *(pattern-interrupt / scroll-stopper)*

**Creative angle / why it works:** Opens on a visual everyone recognizes and dreads — a browser stuffed with tabs — then physically collapses it into one clean product view. The interrupt is the *relief* of the collapse; the scroll stops because the viewer wants to feel that release.

**Platform · format · length:** LinkedIn feed video (also IG Reels) · 9:16 and 1:1 · 12s.

**Hook (first 2 sec):** Extreme close-up of a browser tab bar crammed to illegibility — 40+ funder portals, grant sites, spreadsheets. A cursor hovers, overwhelmed. Text snaps on: **"This is where your opportunity lives right now."**

**Full script / copy (VO):**
- (0–2s) "This is where your opportunity lives right now."
- (2–6s) "Scattered across portals, inboxes, spreadsheets, and memory."
- (6–9s) "Anansi Atlas pulls all of it into one view."
- (9–12s) "The web of opportunity around your mission — mapped."

**On-screen text:** "40 tabs." → *(collapse)* → "One Opportunity Web." → "anansiatlas.com/anansi-atlas/"

**Visual direction:** Cluttered navy-tinted browser chrome. On the beat, all tabs vacuum inward and resolve into the real **Opportunity Web page**: the orb-web hero, "YOUR MISSION" hub at center, six pill nodes (FUNDERS · PARTNERS · GOVERNMENT · RESOURCES · READINESS · PATHWAYS) settling into orbit with gold connector lines. Smooth, weighty transition.

**Music / sound:** Rising tension pad + a rush-of-air "whoosh" on the collapse, resolving to a single clean chime. Calm low pulse underneath.

**VO voice:** Giselle (problem→relief cadence).

**CTA:** "See your web. Founding pilot open now → anansiatlas.com/anansi-atlas/"

**AI video-gen prompt:** "Cinematic 9:16, dark navy and charcoal palette with gold accents. Open on an extreme macro shot of a web browser tab bar overloaded with 40+ tiny illegible tabs, subtle screen glow, shallow depth of field. On a sharp beat, all tabs animate inward with a vacuum/implosion effect and dissolve into a single elegant dashboard: a glowing orb-shaped network diagram, a central hub labeled 'YOUR MISSION', six labeled pill-shaped nodes orbiting it connected by thin gold lines. Premium motion-graphics feel, Fraunces serif title cards, smooth easing, no people, no spider imagery. 12 seconds."

**Difficulty · cost:** Medium · low–mid (motion graphics + one real UI capture).

Cut to a bright, calm morning: the **Snapshot page** — 30-day action plan up top, a rose "Due" deadline pill, teal readiness strength, gold gap. Then the Dashboard "What To Do Next" single-action card resolving into focus.

**Music / sound:** Sparse late-night piano, ticking undertone during agitation; warm swell as the UI appears; resolves to quiet confidence.

**VO voice:** Giselle.

**CTA:** "End the 11pm spiral. Founding seats open → anansiatlas.com/anansi-atlas/"

Abstract, elegant, network/constellation feel. Absolutely no spider or web-as-creature imagery — this is a data constellation.

**Music / sound:** Textured ambient noise that literally "cleans up" into a clear harmonic tone as the particles order themselves.

**VO voice:** Christopher (brand anchor, warm authority).

**CTA:** "Map your mission's web → anansiatlas.com/anansi-atlas/"

## Which 3 to shoot first

Test these three on LinkedIn now — highest impact for the lowest spend, and each probes a different psychological lever so the winners inform everything else:

1. **Concept 11 — "Swipe The Web" (carousel).** Design-only, zero video cost, and the format LinkedIn rewards most. Carries the *full* narrative arc (scattered → mapped → offer → scarcity) in one asset, so it's the fastest way to learn which slides/hooks resonate before spending on video.
2. **Concept 09 — "Six Words" (kinetic typography).** Near-free to produce (no talent, no location, no UI shoot), autoplay-safe muted, and it hammers the six-node vocabulary while testing the pure-brand hook. Ideal cheap scroll-stopper to run against the carousel.
3. **Concept 10 — "Read This With The Sound Off" (silent/captions-first).** Only needs one clean Snapshot screen capture plus caption animation. It's the cheapest way to A/B a *product-forward, muted-feed* cut and see whether real UI or abstract type drives more applies.

Once these three surface a winning hook, graduate the budget to **Concept 02 (11pm Spiral)** or **Concept 06 (Day in the Life)** for the emotional/shot-based tier, and **Concept 12** as the cinematic brand anchor.
