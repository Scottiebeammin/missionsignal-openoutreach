The Problem

Most opportunity work is spread across inboxes, relationships, documents, deadlines, and memory.

That makes leadership decisions harder: which funder is worth pursuing, which relationship matters now, which gap will block progress, and what action should happen this week?

The Web of Opportunity

A leadership map for the whole opportunity ecosystem, not another list of possible grants.

The Web of Opportunity connects mission, programs, funders, government pathways, partners, resources, documents, risks, deadlines, relationships, and wins so teams can focus on the moves with the highest leverage.
