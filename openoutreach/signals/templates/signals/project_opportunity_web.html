What is the Opportunity Web?

Every mission is surrounded by opportunity — the funders, partners, government pathways, and resources connected to your work, plus the readiness you need to reach them. The Opportunity Web maps all of it in one place and shows how the pieces connect — so instead of a scattered list, you see the whole landscape at once: where your strongest opportunities are, what's holding you back, and the single highest-leverage move to make next.
