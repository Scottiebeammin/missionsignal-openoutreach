Pilot FAQ

Clear expectations before you raise your hand.

What is Anansi Atlas?

Anansi Atlas is an Opportunity Command Center for seeing funders, partners, relationships, readiness gaps, resources, pathways, and next actions around a mission.

What is an Opportunity Web Snapshot?

It is a consulting-style executive summary of your Opportunity Web, including top opportunities, relationship gaps, readiness gaps, strategic moves, and a 30-day action plan.

How long does the pilot last?

The pilot includes 30 days of platform access after the walkthrough.

Who is this for?

Nonprofits, workforce organizations, community initiatives, consultants, and mission-driven teams that want clearer opportunity strategy.

What happens after the pilot?

We review feedback, identify what changed for your organization, and discuss whether continued access makes sense.

Do I need technical experience?

No. The pilot is founder-led and designed for executive, development, program, and strategy leaders.

How much time is required?

Plan for one intake, one walkthrough, and focused use during the 30-day action period.
