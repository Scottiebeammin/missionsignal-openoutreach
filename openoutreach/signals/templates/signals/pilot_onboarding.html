Questions

Clear expectations before you raise your hand.

What is Anansi Atlas?

An Opportunity Command Center for seeing funders, partners, relationships, readiness gaps, resources, and next actions around your mission.

What is an Opportunity Web Snapshot?

A consulting-style executive brief including top opportunities, relationship and readiness gaps, strategic moves, and a 30-day action plan.

How long does the pilot last?

30 days of platform access after your founder-led walkthrough.

Do I need technical experience?

No. The pilot is designed for executive, development, program, and strategy leaders — no technical background needed.

How much time is required?

Plan for one intake session, one walkthrough, and focused use during the 30-day action period.

What happens after the pilot?

We review what changed for your organization and discuss whether continued access makes sense for your team.
